About DanceBeat

DanceBeat Hip-Hop Dance Festival takes place every November, bringing together over 2000 hip-hop lovers from all over the world. Koana Hip-Hop Dance Festival was kicked off in 2003 by Mexican American breakdancer Lilu Boja and has grown to become one of the world's most well-known hip-hop dance festivals.
